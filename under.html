THE Undertaker

summary

Mark William Calaway(born March 24, 1965) , better known by the ring name The Undertaker, is an American retired professional wrestler.
Widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time,[11] Calaway spent the vast majority of his career wrestling for WWF/E, and is best known for his critically acclaimed "Deadman" Undertaker gimmick, for which he was inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2022.[12
